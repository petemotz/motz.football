Positions

Abbey Villa Soccer Club

  • Position: Boys U11+ Technical Director
  • Tenure: January 2014 - Present

Abbey Villa Soccer Club is a club located in Massachusetts, serving central and northern parts of the state, playing in the New England Premiership. The club believes that all players should be developed into technically proficient players.

    Responsibilities:
  • Began as coach in January 2014, promoted to Boys Director in May 2015.
  • Develop and deliver technically and tactically based training sessions.
  • Oversee the development of all boys (350+) at the club.
  • Supervise and educate coaches ensuring they are adhering to the club philosophy during training sessions and game day.
  • Responsible for soccer and futsal curriculum development and coaching.
  • Represent the club at league meetings.
  • In charge of scheduling training and home league game fixtures.
  • Create imagery and graphics for announcements and advertisement needs.
  • Maintain social media accounts: Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
  • Helped organize a 10 day trip to Ireland for a Boys Under-14 team, playing against the likes of St. Kevin's and training with Cherry Orchard. An Interview about the experience.
  • Won three Massachusetts State Cups with U12, U15, U16 Boys teams and organised trip to Region 1 Championships in West Virginia.

Newbury College

Boston University

  • Position: Men's Club Soccer Coach
  • Tenure: August 2013 - May 2015

Boston University's Club Soccer team was recognized by the unversity in 2013. The team participates in NIRSA, an organization that oversees a national network of collegiate club teams.

    Responsibilities:
  • Began coaching the team in what was the team’s first officially recognized season by Boston University under their PERD department.
  • Designed and delivered three training sessions per week.
  • Participated in club board meetings and assisted in decision making.
  • Helped team create fundraising and volunteer opportunities throughout Boston.
  • Guided team to NIRSA East Division title twice and the New England Conference Tournament title; semifinalists at the NIRSA Region 1 tournament.
  • Helped organize two trips to Pennsylvania and Delaware for the NIRSA Region 1 tournaments.
